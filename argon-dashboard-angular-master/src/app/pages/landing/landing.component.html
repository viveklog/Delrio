Automated Radio Frequency Identification done in minutes
The Del Rio Pass program employs cutting-edge radio-frequency identification (RFID) technology to accurately identify member vehicles and seamlessly deduct toll charges from their pre-paid accounts. Applying for an account is conveniently available either through this website or in person at the esteemed Del Rio Pass Office located at the International Bridge.
Get your Del Rio Pass
All data collected and presented at one place
As you approach the toll lane, please drive at a reduced speed as toll collectors and maintenance staff frequently move in the area for safety reasons. Once your Del Rio Pass transponder is detected by the antenna near the toll booth, the traffic light will turn green and the gate will open automatically if your account is in good standing. However, if there are any issues with your account, such as insufficient funds, the traffic light will remain red, and you will be required to pay the toll collector in cash.
Automated Identification
As you enter the toll lane, a sleek antenna perched near the toll booth scans the details encrypted within your mighty Del Rio Pass transponder.
Both credit card or cash can be used
Customers have the flexibility to fuel their accounts with either credit card or cash, while a toll amount is promptly calculated and deducted from your Del Rio Pass account's prepaid balance upon each bridge crossing.
Amount Auto added to the account
As the balance of your Del Rio Pass account approaches the "low threshold," it will seamlessly recharge from your registered credit card, while cash-paying customers must visit the Del Rio Pass office at the International Bridge to add funds to their respective accounts.
Easy Installation
Transponders come with clear and user-friendly installation instructions for effortless setup.
